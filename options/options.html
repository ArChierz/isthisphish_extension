IsThisPhish?

Machine Learning-Based Chrome Extension for Phishing Website Detection.
This project has been made to fulfil the requirement for bachelor thesis and only cover the basic necessity.
Further development and research is needed to improve the accuracy and precision to detect phishing site and reduce false positive.
Here provided a whitelist feature to list legitimate site that may be blocked by the extension.
